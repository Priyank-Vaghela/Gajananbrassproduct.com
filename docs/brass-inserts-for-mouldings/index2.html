CNC VMC Machine Turned Parts

CNC VMC machine turned Variable parts are available as per customer’s design and samples

The CNC machined components manufactured are always checked for quality with various gauges and sophisticated instruments. It involves turning on CNC and drilling on VMC. It undergoes special process of nickel plating and on turning special lip shaped profile is made at a high depth using world class specialized tooling. The different sizes of bore are made having close tolerances that are controlled by our effective quality systems.
